BOI-BEXY

BOI-BEXY is a Lagos-born Afrobeat artist known for his viral hits and energetic performances. From the streets of Lagos to the global stage, BOI-BEXY’s journey reflects his passion, talent, and drive to put African music on the map. With hit tracks like 'Gbemisoke', he’s just getting started.
